# Aurelius Target Market and GTM Strategy

## 1. Strategic Position
Aurelius is positioned as an operational intelligence layer above existing HR suites, not a rip-and-replace HRIS.

## 2. Target Customer Segments
1. Enterprise with fragmented HR stack:
   - Has Workday/SAP/Oracle + ATS + engagement platforms.
2. High-attrition operations:
   - Retail, logistics, healthcare frontline, BPO.
3. Talent-dense organizations:
   - Tech and knowledge firms needing mobility and succession intelligence.

## 3. Buying Triggers
1. Rising attrition and backfill delays.
2. CFO pressure on workforce cost efficiency.
3. Leadership demand for predictive planning.
4. Compliance and audit pressure for AI decisions.

## 4. Entry Strategy
1. Land with one high-value use case:
   - Attrition command center + intervention workflow.
2. Expand to:
   - Internal mobility and recruiting optimization.
3. Platformize:
   - Workforce economics and scenario planning.

## 5. Package Design (Recommended)
1. `Aurelius Core`:
   - Risk, dashboards, interventions, audit.
2. `Aurelius Talent`:
   - Matching, mobility, TA intelligence.
3. `Aurelius Economics`:
   - Forecasts, optimizer, finance-grade scenarios.
4. `Aurelius Governance`:
   - Compliance packs, fairness and policy controls.

## 6. Deployment Model
1. Enterprise SaaS (default).
2. Private VPC deployment (regulated industries).
3. Regional data residency options.

## 7. 12-Month Market Attack Plan
1. Quarter 1:
   - 3 lighthouse customers, one vertical each.
2. Quarter 2:
   - Publish quantified case studies (attrition and time-to-fill impact).
3. Quarter 3:
   - Expand integrations and partner channel.
4. Quarter 4:
   - Board-level workforce planning modules.

## 8. Sales and Success Motions
1. Value engineering:
   - baseline customer metrics before rollout.
2. 90-day ROI plan:
   - measurable targets per customer.
3. Executive review cadence:
   - monthly impact and intervention outcomes.

## 9. Core Proof Points Required for Enterprise Wins
1. Measurable reduction in preventable attrition.
2. Faster and better hiring decisions.
3. Clear financial impact and cost avoidance.
4. Audit-ready governance for AI recommendations.
